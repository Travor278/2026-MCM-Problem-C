After analyzing all 34 seasons, we present key findings and recommendations for combining judge scores and fan votes.

\section*{Key Findings}

\noindent\begin{minipage}[t]{0.54\textwidth}
\textbf{1. Scoring Method Comparison:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Percentage-based}: Keeps fan-favorites competitive even with lower technical scores
    \item \textbf{Rank-based}: Amplifies small gaps, reducing fan influence by 13\%
\end{itemize}

\textbf{2. Bottom-2 Mechanism:} Reduces upsets from 42\% to 8\%, preventing outcomes like Bobby Bones (S27).

\textbf{3. Celebrity Type:} Athletes get 13\% more fan votes but 0.15 points lower judge scores --- fans and judges value different qualities.

\textbf{4. Professional Partner:} Pro dancers account for 18.5\% of judge score variance but only 7\% of fan vote variance.
\end{minipage}%

\section*{Recommendations}

\noindent\textbf{1. Keep Percentage-Based Scoring} --- Maintains voting suspense and fan engagement.

\textbf{2. Use Dynamic Judge Weights} --- Judges' influence grows through the season:

\vspace{0.1cm}
\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{cccc}
\toprule
\textbf{Stage} & \textbf{Judge Weight} & \textbf{Metric} & \textbf{Improvement} \\
\midrule
Early (Wk 1--4) & 20\% & Upsets & $-$17\% \\
Mid (Wk 5--8) & 35\% & Engagement & $+$22\% \\
Late (Wk 9+) & 60\% & Fairness & 96\% \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\textbf{3. Keep Bottom-2 from Week 7} --- Creates drama while preventing anomalies.

\textbf{4. Consider Vote Credits} --- Multiple votes for one contestant cost more credits, discouraging mass voting.

\section*{Bottom Line}

Our system increases engagement by \textbf{22\%} while ensuring finals reward true talent. This prevents future controversies while keeping DWTS exciting.
